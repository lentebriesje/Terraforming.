You have a survival gamemode. The quality of the survival experience is very much depending on the quality of the terrain. Terrain generation mods bring custom flair... Terrain like this does not have to be expensive. Hear me out!

Spend less

If you and other server owners are willing to all pay a little, you can have top quality terrain. Not once, but often. What I propose is simple. A terrain creation service that you subscribe to. I will deliver top quality World-Machine made custom terrain at regular intervals for a predetermined amount of money. This same terrain will be available to all servers that opt-in. Everyone pays the same low amount and gets the same top quality terrain.

Make more

An engaged player spends more time on your server. The more time someone invest on your server, the more willing they are to donate to keep it up and running.

Unique and new terrain motivates players to spend more time on your server. Better maps equals a greater willingness to donate.
